-----------------
Third conditional.
-----------------
We are speaking about imagined situations in the past. Cause and result in the past.
Structure: if + (clause: past perfect), would + have +(result: past participle).
If you had invited me, I would have been at your party.
If I had been aware of the right time, I couldn't have been on time.
If you had proposed to me to conduct the lesson, I would have done it.

-----------------
Third conditional, inverted:
-----------------
Structure: had + (clause: past perfect), would/could/should + have +(result: past participle).
Had you invited me, I would have been at your party.
Had I been aware of the right time, I couldn't have been on time.
Had you proposed to me to conduct the lesson, I would have done it.
