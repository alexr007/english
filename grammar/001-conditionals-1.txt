------------------
First conditional:
------------------
We are referring to possible situations in the present or the future.
Structure: if + (clause: present simple), will +(result: future simple).
If we have enough time, we will go to the cinema.
If he works harder, he will win that battle.
If I work better, I will earn more money.

------------------
First conditional, inverted:
------------------
Structure: Should + (clause: present simple), will +(result: future simple)
Should we have enough time, we will go to the cinema.
Should he train harder, he will win this battle.
Should I spend more time, I will earn more money.
